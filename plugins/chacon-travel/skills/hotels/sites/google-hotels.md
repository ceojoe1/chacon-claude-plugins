# Google Hotels — UI Navigation

URL: https://www.google.com/travel/hotels

## Steps

1. Navigate to the URL. Origin and dates may be pre-filled from recent searches — verify and clear if needed.
2. **Destination**: Click the search field and type the destination city (e.g., "Orlando, FL"). Select from the dropdown.
3. **Dates**: Click the check-in field. A calendar opens — navigate with ">" (one click per month). Click check-in date, then check-out date.
4. **Guests/Rooms**: Click the guests field. A picker opens with "+" buttons for Adults and Rooms. **Faster**: use `javascript_tool` to set counts directly rather than clicking "+" repeatedly.
5. Click "Search".

## Reading Results

- Prices shown are **per night per room** — multiply by number of nights and rooms for total stay cost.
- Results show a mix of hotels sorted by relevance. Switch to "Lowest price" sort for best deals.
- Star ratings and guest scores (out of 5 or 10) are shown per listing.
- Google Hotels links out to third-party booking sites — note the booking site shown for each price.
