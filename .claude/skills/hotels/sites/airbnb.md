# Airbnb — UI Navigation

URL: https://www.airbnb.com

## Steps

1. Navigate to the URL.
2. **Destination**: Click the "Where" field and type the city or area. Select from dropdown.
3. **Dates**: Click "Check in" and select from the calendar; click "Check out" and select the return date.
4. **Guests**: Click "Who" and use "+" to set Adults count. **Faster**: use `javascript_tool` to set directly.
5. Click "Search".

## Reading Results

- Prices are shown **per night** before taxes/fees. The total (with fees) is shown on the listing detail page.
- Airbnb shows entire homes, private rooms, and shared spaces — filter by "Entire home" for group trips.
- Look at the total price (including cleaning fee and service fee) before comparing — nightly rates can be misleading.
- "Guest favorite" badge indicates highly rated properties.
